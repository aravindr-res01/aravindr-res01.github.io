Explainable AI for Automated Compliance and Regulatory Reporting in FinTech Systems

Accepted / In Publishing

Authors: Aravind Raghu

Type: Conference Paper

Venue: International Conference on Computational Science and Computational Intelligence (CSCI 2025)

Year: 2025

Abstract

This paper introduces an explainable AI framework for automated compliance and regulatory reporting in FinTech environments. The work focuses on improving transparency in machine learning-assisted compliance pipelines so that financial institutions can benefit from automation while preserving interpretability for auditors, regulators, and internal stakeholders.

The proposed approach integrates explainable models and traceable decision workflows into compliance reporting systems, helping organizations improve accountability, reduce manual effort, and strengthen trust in AI-supported regulatory operations.

The study demonstrates the importance of explainability in high-stakes financial systems where compliance outcomes must remain understandable, defensible, and aligned with regulatory expectations.

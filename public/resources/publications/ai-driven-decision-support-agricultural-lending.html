Implementing AI-Driven Decision Support in Agricultural Lending Through Predictive Analytics for Customer Relationship Management

Authors: Karthik Nallani Chakravartula, Aravind Raghu

Type: Journal Article

Venue: Journal of Intelligent Management Decision

Year: 2026

DOI: 10.56578/jimd050102

Abstract

This study explores the implementation of AI-driven decision support in agricultural lending through predictive analytics integrated with customer relationship management systems. The framework is designed to help lenders evaluate borrower risk, historical repayment patterns, and relevant agricultural factors more effectively.

By incorporating predictive intelligence into lending workflows, the approach aims to improve the quality of credit decisions, increase operational efficiency, and support more data-driven customer relationship management in agricultural finance environments.

The work highlights the potential of AI-enabled CRM systems to strengthen lending accuracy and improve decision support capabilities for institutions serving agriculture-focused borrowers.
